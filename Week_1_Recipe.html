Baked Sweet Potato Fries

How to bake and enjoy fries guilt free

Photo Credit: Physicians Committee

    Ingredients

  • 2 Sweet Potatoes that have been boiled and cut into slices
  • 3 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp paprika

    Steps To Follow

  1. Preheat Oven to 425 Degree Farenheight
  2. In a bowl, mix all your ingredients
  3. Spread on a baking tray lined with parchment paper
  4. Bake for 25-30 min or until golden brown.
